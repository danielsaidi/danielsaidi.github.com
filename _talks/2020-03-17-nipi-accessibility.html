--- title: "Improving Accessibility in a Popular Audio Book Service" date: 2020-02-11 location: Nipi - Nordic Conference on Inclusive Publishing abstract: | Most software companies want their products and services to be as accessible as possible to as many as possible. However, while many developer tools have good accessibility support, many non-technical challenges still stand in the way of creating accessible products. A lack of area expertise, resources and routines can make even the best of intentions fail.
In this talk, I discuss how BookBeat worked together with MTM to improve the internal accessibility focus, as well as remaining challenges and some ideas for the future. ---
