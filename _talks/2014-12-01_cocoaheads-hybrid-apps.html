--- title: "Hybrid Apps" date: 2014-12-01 location: CocoaHeads Stockholm redirect_to: /talks/custom/2014-12-01_cocoaheads-hybrid-apps abstract: | In this talk, I describe how Apple's initial vision of 3rd party iOS apps went from mobile web browser experiences to the native apps we have today. I then go through a case study of how a Swedish high-profile company did replace their native apps with custom-made, hybrid apps that wrapped their new responsive web site. ---
